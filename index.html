Features

Wooden Frame

The strong, wooden frame and top carriage serves as the sorter's foundation.

Rack & Pinion System

The sorter utilizes a 3D-printed rack and pinion system powered by a motor to move across bins.

Servo-Powered Flaps

To drop the waste inside the bin, flaps on the box open and close using servos and their positions.

Hinged Door

For user convenience, the hinged door is attracted to magnets, so the door closes easily and hits a limit switch.

Electronics

The electronics consist of the Raspberry Pi, motor controller, camera, limit switch, power supply, and power switch.

TensorFlow Model

The sorter uses an object detection TensorFlow model with high accuracy to detect the image as landfill, recycling, or compost.
